Education

UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta is a higher education institution with an Islamic, modern and Indonesian perspective. The establishment of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta was the result of the struggle of Muslims Indonesia must have higher education institutions that can galvanize their students to become reliable cadres of the community who can respond to the needs of society and changing times.

Facility

Harun Nasution Auditorium

This auditorium has an artistic design and can accommodate around a thousand people. This auditorium is often used for various events such as seminars, workshops and graduation processions.

Student Center

This building is a gathering place for students and there is a mini stadium, field, mosque, hall, cooperative and UKM secretariat.

Cangkir cafe

This cafe provides various kinds of food menus such as rice, noodles, chicken, fish and other side dishes. This cafe is also equipped with a minimarket that sells various student needs.
