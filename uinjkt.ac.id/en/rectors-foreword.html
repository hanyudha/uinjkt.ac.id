About Syarif Hidayatullah Islamic University Jakarta

Rector's Foreword

WELCOME

Welcome to Syarif Hidayatullah State Islamic University (UIN) Jakarta, where you can start making real changes.

As one of the leading Islamic universities in Indonesia, UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta strives to facilitate the young generation from all over the country and the world to develop themselves and maximize their potential. We are committed to providing this dynamic and passionate community with quality education for a better tomorrow.

The advantages of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta cover a wide spectrum of fields. There are more than 84 study programs at various levels of study and professions as well as 20 research and community service centers that will help students broaden their horizons and enrich their experiences in research, interdisciplinary collaboration and life in general.

UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta has an extensive partnership network with national and global educational institutions, research institutions, government institutions, NGOs and industry. We synergize in education, knowledge exchange, technology transfer, and more. Currently, UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta has graduates who collaborate with various well-known universities in the world.

Our campus is located on the border of DKI Jakarta and Banten Provinces, a city famous for Islamic thoughts, namely Ciputat, South Tangerang City. Therefore, not only an academic experience, here, whoever you are, wherever you come from, can experience firsthand a rich intercultural experience. We invite you to study at our inclusive campus, with Islamic and Indonesian nuances, where we can work together to create a real impact on the nation and the world.

Thank you for visiting our page. We hope that the UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta campus will give you a sweet impression.

Ciputat, March 1 2023

 

Rector,
Prof. Asep Saepudin Jahar, MA, Ph.D
