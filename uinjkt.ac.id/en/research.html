Research

UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta has a research center which is tasked with providing guidance and development of research in various fields of science, such as religious, economic and social sciences, science and technology, psychology education, medicine and health sciences. This research center plays a role in achieving the vision, mission and objectives of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta to become a World Class University.

Institute for Research and Community Service

The Institute for Research and Community Service, hereinafter referred to as LP2M, is an academic implementation element that carries out some of the duties and functions of the Institute in the field of research and community service. As stated in the REGULATION OF THE MINISTER OF AGAMAREPUBLIC OF INDONESIA NUMBER 17 OF 2014 CONCERNING THE STATUTE OF THE SYARIF HIDAYATULLAH STATE ISLAMIC UNIVERSITY OF JAKARTA, LP2M has the task of carrying out, coordinating, monitoring and assessing research and community service activities based on the Chancellor's policy.

UIN Press

UIN Press was founded with the aim of providing a solution as the right publisher for writers, both lecturers and students in the PTKIN environment at the State Islamic University of Jakarta and in general the Indonesian people in accordance with the needs and desires of writers related to printing and publishing.
